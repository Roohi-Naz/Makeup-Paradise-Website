ORDER AND PAYMENT

Placing an Order:

At Makeup Paradise, ordering your favorite products is a breeze. Here’s how:

  1. Browse the Catalog: Explore our extensive collection of cosmetics, skincare, and accessories.
  2. Add to Cart: Select the items you love and add them to your shopping cart.
  3. Checkout: Proceed to checkout when you’re ready to place your order.

Payment Options:

We offer multiple secure payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Pay conveniently using your Visa, Mastercard, or other major credit/debit cards.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Prefer paying upon delivery? Opt for COD.
  • Mobile Wallets: Use popular mobile wallets for seamless transactions.

Order Confirmation:

After placing your order, you’ll receive an order confirmation email. This email includes details such as your order number, items purchased, and delivery address.

Tracking Your Order:

Stay informed about your order’s status:

  • Order Tracking: Once your order is dispatched, we’ll provide a tracking number. You can monitor your package’s journey.
  • Delivery Updates: Receive notifications via email or SMS as your order progresses.

Secure Transactions:

Your privacy matters to us. PinkFlash ensures secure transactions through encryption protocols. Your payment details are safe with us.

Returns and Refunds:

Not satisfied with your purchase? No worries! We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Contact our customer support team for assistance.
Remember, at Makeup Paradise, we’re committed to making your shopping experience delightful from order placement to delivery!
