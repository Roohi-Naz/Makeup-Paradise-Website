SHIPPING AND DELIVERY

Shipping Options:

Makeup Paradise offers flexible shipping options to cater to your needs:

  • Standard Shipping: Our standard shipping ensures reliable delivery within 3-5 business days.
  • Express Shipping: Need your products sooner? Opt for express shipping, which delivers within 1-2 business days.

Delivery Charges:

We believe in transparency. Here’s how our delivery charges work:

  • Orders below 5k PKR: A flat delivery charge of 200 PKR applies.
  • Orders above 5k PKR: Enjoy free shipping!

Packaging and Handling:

We take utmost care in packaging your orders securely. Our team ensures that your Makeup Paradise products arrive in pristine condition. Additionally, we’ve transitioned to eco-friendly plastic packaging to reduce our environmental impact.

Tracking Your Order:

Stay informed! Once your order is dispatched, we’ll provide a tracking number. You can monitor your package’s journey from our warehouse to your doorstep.

Delivery Times:

  • Local Deliveries: Expect your order within 3-5 business days.
  • International Deliveries: Delivery times vary based on the destination. We’ll provide estimated delivery dates during checkout.

Returns and Exchanges:

Not satisfied with your purchase? No worries! Makeup Paradise accepts returns and exchanges within 14 days of delivery. Just reach out to our customer support team.
Remember, at Makeup Paradise, we’re committed to delivering beauty right to your doorstep. Happy shopping!
